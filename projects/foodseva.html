Food Seva

  • Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL

Developed a platform to collect excess food from donors and redistribute it to people in need, reducing food waste and helping thousands of individuals.

Implemented a MySQL database to manage donor and recipient information, and created an interactive website using HTML, CSS, and PHP to allow users to sign up, donate food, and request food assistance.

The platform has helped reduce food waste by 30% and has benefited over 2,000 individuals in need, allowing them to receive nutritious meals.

Key features of the platform include:

  • Donor Registration
  • Food Donation Form
  • Recipient Request System
  • Admin Dashboard to Manage Donations
